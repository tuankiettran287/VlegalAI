# Icon Attribution

The icon-enhanced renders are generated from these Iconify collections:

- `@iconify-json/logos`
- `@iconify-json/simple-icons`
- `@iconify-json/lucide`

Lucide icons are provided under the ISC License. Simple Icons data is provided
under CC0 1.0; individual brands and trademarks remain the property of their
respective owners. The Iconify Logos collection aggregates project and product
marks for identification purposes.

The generated SVG and PNG diagrams embed the required vector paths and make no
runtime request to an icon CDN.
